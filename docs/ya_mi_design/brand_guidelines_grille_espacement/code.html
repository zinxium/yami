Design Foundations

Visual Harmony & The Spacing System

Our layout philosophy prioritizes 'Espace Respirant'—breathing space—to ensure that even complex financial data feels approachable and premium.

The 8px Base Unit

All spacing, from button padding to section margins, is a multiple of 8px. This creates a mathematical rhythm that the human eye perceives as stable and reliable.
